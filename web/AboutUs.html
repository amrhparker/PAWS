About Us

What is PAWS?

PAWS is a digital adoption management system designed to connect hopeful adopters with pets in need of loving homes. The system helps streamline the adoption process from application to final adoption.

Our Mission

We aim to ensure safe, responsible adoption through proper documentation, transparent review processes, and follow-up reporting to ensure the pet's well-being.
